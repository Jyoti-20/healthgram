Our Purpose


Virtual blood bank

It supplies people information about the availability of potential donors and blood banks near them.

Donor will donate the blood to the blood bank and the hospital will also register the donor for future. The blood type of the donor will go under several tests in the hospital to check if it is suitable for other patients.

As we have seen in the last years, people faced a alot of problems at the time of covid-19 pandemic due to shortage of various blood in blood banks.So, our website would be very helpful in such situations of emergency.

Disease Predictor

Nowadays, humans face various diseases due to the current environmental conditions and their living habits. The identification and prediction of such diseases at their earlier stages are much important, so as to prevent the extremity of it.

Disease predictor is used to predict the diseases from the symptoms which are given by the patients or any user. It would process the symptoms provided by the user as input and would give the output as the probability of the disease.

So, our main objective is to develop a web interface platform for the prediction of the disease.

Our Objective


Virtual blood bank

Its aim is to solve the problems of the patients in the shortest possible time.

Any healthy person interested in donating blood can register themselves in the hospital near them and the hospital will be registered under the site and provide details like name, age, blood group and contact number, of the donor registered under that hospital that are available for donation of blood to the patient. A needy patient or a relative can access the site and put in a request. An SMS is then sent to the donor located nearest to the hospital where the patient is.The patient or his relative is also informed about the identity of the donor.
